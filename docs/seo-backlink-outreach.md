# Backlink Outreach Scaffolding

A starting list of sites that align with this content and short email templates
the site owner can adapt. Outreach is a manual, ongoing task — these are drafts,
not automated sends. Keep the tone factual and educational, matching the site.

## Target categories

**Hawaii news organizations** — Maui Now, Honolulu Civil Beat, Star-Advertiser,
Hawaii News Now, The Maui News.

**National fire service publications** — Fire Engineering, Firehouse,
FireRescue1, IAFF national newsroom.

**Labor & policy** — IAFF (iaff.org), state labor federations, labor-law and
public-policy blogs that cover the FLSA 7(k) exemption.

**Academic / reference** — researchers and explainer sites that cite fireground
staffing studies (NIST/UL crew-size research, NFPA 1710) or FLSA overtime law.

---
