### B — Federal 7(k) overtime exemption (Overtime Law)
Owns: FLSA/7(k) mechanics + history (1938/1974/1985 Garcia); 160 vs 212 hr thresholds; 53-hr week; **optional** nature; Kelly-no-Kelly-days schedule; 24/72 alternative; the foregone-OT table.
Key claims: 212 hrs/28 days ≈ 53 hr/wk; Maui Kelly = 121–122 shifts/yr, ~56.3 hr/wk, ~225 hr/cycle; loss ~$1,463/cycle, ~$19,013/yr, ~$475,313/25-yr at $45/hr; all 4 counties apply it.
Sources: 29 U.S.C. §207(k), §203(y); 29 C.F.R. Part 553; DOL Fact Sheets 8 & 17J; Garcia; UNC SOG; TN CTAS; IAFF; firefighterovertime.org; DHRD BU-11; Maui rep.
